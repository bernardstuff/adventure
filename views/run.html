Duckey On The Run.

Duckey left his mum and ran deep into the jungle. It was there that he ran into a biochemist named Dr. Schwarzenegger.

The good doctor took great care of him and later introduced him to his son Pete. Pete and Duckey were inseperable and grew up living in the same room recievieng the same gifts on Christmas and on Pete's birthday.
Life was Good for a while.

One day Dr. Schwarzenegger accidentally brought home an experimental drug.

Long story short, Duckey turned planet Earth into the "Planet of The Apes", he also kept Pete and Dr. Schwarzenegger as his advisers.
